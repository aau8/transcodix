Content delivery network (CDN)

Content delivery network (CDN) consists of a group of geographically distributed and interconnected servers. That helps to improve the perfomanse of life video streaming by reducing the time required to send content to final users.

One of the most important features of CDN is security. CDN helps protect data with TLS/SSL certificates to guarantee a high standard of encryption, authentication and integrity.
